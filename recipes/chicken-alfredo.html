Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a widely loved dish that comes together fairly quick. Simple ingredients all in one pot make for an easy cleanup as well!

Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 16oz Pasta of your choice (I prefer penne)
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 4 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 8oz Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

  1. Bring a large of pot of salted water to a boil. Add your pasta and cook it according to the instructions on the box. Reserve some pasta water, about half a cup to add to the sauce later.
  2. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook chicken for 5-7 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165F. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Turn down the heat to medium. Add the butter follwed by the minced garlic to the pan and cook until fragrant. Add the heavy cream and pasta water then simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Once the butter and cream have gotten to know each other, add the parmesan, whisking occasionally to incorporate fully.
  5. Let the sauce simmer on medium heat for 7-8 minutes or until thickened.
  6. Add the chicken and pasta to the sauce and toss together. Serve in bowls with more parmesan if desired.
